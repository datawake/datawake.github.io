Jennifer Nicolaisen

Principal Consultant

Download CV

Jennifer Nicolaisen demonstrates a strong track-record of catalysing organizational change through intentional and effective communication. A graduate of the University of North Carolina in Chapel Hill with her degree in Linguistics, Jennifer leveraged her skills consulting to Fortune 1000 companies in energy, pharma, chemical, and manufacturing sectors to ensure effective planning and execution of major capital projects. Since then, she's shifted focus to guiding more personal, foundational change for individuals, company leaders, and business teams of all shapes and sizes. Jennifer is the founder of the Datawake Consultancy.
